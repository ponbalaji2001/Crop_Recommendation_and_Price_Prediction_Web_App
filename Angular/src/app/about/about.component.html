Challenges in Agriculture

In this world, the need for food and nourishment surpasses all other needs. Most of the food production is contributed by agriculture. Therefore, agriculture is at the heart of the economy in any country. Especially in India due to increasing population the thirst for latest technologies is imminent. Accurate predictions of crop yields and prices are required by farmers in general to make educated decisions on planting, harvesting, and marketing their crops.

The traditional or existing system of agriculture faces a major issue, that is, fluctuation in the prices of crop produce. The rise in prices leads to increased cost of living and the fall in prices leads to increased profit. Due to its unpredictable nature, it might lead to problems such as investment loss. Moreover, most of the farmers are unaware of the market demand and face difficulties in marketing their product. Decades ago, naked eye observation and instinct were enough to predict the weather but nowadays due to globalization, the weather has become unpredictable. Hence another issue lies in predicting weather conditions, which can affect the yield drastically. Inaccurate predictions can pose a danger to the food security of any country.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower farmers with accurate and timely information to help them make informed decisions about their crops. By utilizing advanced machine learning algorithms and real-time agro-meteorological data, we provide farmers with precise crop recommendations based on their local soil moisture, rainfall, temperature, and other parameters.

In addition, we offer real-time crop price predictions, enabling farmers to stay updated with the market demand and trends and make informed decisions about when to sell their crops to obtain maximum profit. Our ultimate goal is to help farmers increase their yield and profitability, while minimizing their crop loss and investment risk.
